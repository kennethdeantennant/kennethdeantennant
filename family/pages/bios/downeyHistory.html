Family History of Cheryl Tennant
Downey
The Downey line has been traced back to William Downey, Jr. who was born in Brockville, Ontario, Canada. William was married to Sarah Hindmarsh 1 Januray 1862. William's father, William Downey Sr. possibly was born in Scotland about 1804.

 

Synder
The Snyder line has been traced back to Conrad Bartholemew Snyder who was born 4 jan 1873 in Jefferson City, Missouri. He married Laura Idella Slates 10 Dec 1899. Conrad's father, John Nicklos Snyder or Schneider, was born 2 October 1837 in Bavaria, Bonn, Germany.

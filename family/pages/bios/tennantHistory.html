Family History of Mike Tennant
Tennant
According to historical records, the name comes from Scotland. In fact, the same history states that all Tennant ancestors and their various spelling forms originated from Scotland. However, this particular line has only been traced back to John William Tennant, who immigrated from Canada to Wisconsin before 1844. It is believed that John William or William John immigrated once more to Missouri and died in 1889. This informaton can be found in an 1880 Missouri census record showing that his oldest son, William Robert, was born in Portage, Wisconsin in 1844.

 

Neilson
The name can be traced back to Sweden to an individual by the name of Mattias Larsson who was born in 1716. The name first shows up as Neilson when Carl Peter Neilson, born 1859 changed the spelling of his last name, Nilsson, when he immagrated to America in 1889.
